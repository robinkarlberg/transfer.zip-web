Data Security

At Transfer.zip, we prioritize the security and privacy of our users. We have implemented measures to ensure the protection of your data, although we do not collect or store any user information on our servers. Our website operates in a client-side manner, which means that all data processing and storage occur locally on your device.

As a result, we do not have access to or control over any data you generate or manipulate on our website. This approach eliminates the need for us to implement specific security measures for user data, as no information is transmitted or stored on our servers. Any data you handle on Transfer.zip remains within the confines of your device and browser.

While we take reasonable steps to ensure the security of our website and maintain a safe browsing environment, it is important to remember that the security of your device and browser settings are ultimately your responsibility. We recommend keeping your operating system, browser, and security software up to date to minimize potential risks.
